## Backlog Discipline

Before starting any task, check **`../docs/BACKLOG.md`**. After completing or deferring work:
- **Tick off** any backlog item your change addresses (move it to the Completed table).
- **Add a new entry** if you are consciously deferring something — capture what it is, why it's deferred, and which files are relevant.
- **Do not implement** stretch features or hardening that isn't needed right now. Defer it and document it instead.
